About Us

Welcome to Pizza Time Pizzeria! Since 1998, we've been serving up the freshest, most delicious pizzas in town. Our dough is made daily, our sauces are crafted from secret family recipes, and every pizza is baked to perfection in our wood-fired oven. Whether you're here for a family dinner or a quick lunch, we promise a warm atmosphere and unforgettable flavors!
